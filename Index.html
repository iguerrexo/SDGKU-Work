Banana Pancakes

"A simple crowd pleasing recipe made from scratch. A fun twist on ordinary pancakes."

Prep Time 5 Minutes | Cook 10 Minutes | Ready in 15 Minutes |

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 cup of unsweetend vanilla almond milk
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 ripe bannanas

Directions:

  1. Combine flour, white sugar, baking powder and salt. In a separate bowl, mix together egg, milk, vegetable oil, bananas and 1 cup all-purpose flour
  2. Stir flour mixture into banana mixture; batter will be slightly lumpy.
  3. Heat a lightly oiled griddle/frying pan over medium high heat.
  4. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake.
  5. Cook until pancakes are golden brown on both sides; serve hot.
